# Privacy Policy for XPlay

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **XPlay: Video Downloader for X & Twitter** ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. No Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

This extension operates entirely locally on your device.

- **No Analytics:** We do not track your usage or clicks.
- **No Accounts:** You do not need to create an account to use this extension.
- **No External Servers:** Video data is processed directly within your browser. We do not send your browsing history or downloaded video URLs to any external servers.

## 3. Permissions Usage

We require specific permissions solely for the extension to function:

- **Host Permissions (`*://*.twitter.com/*`, `*://*.x.com/*`)**: Used only to detect video elements on the X/Twitter page and inject the download button.
- **Storage**: Used locally to remember your simple preference settings (if any).

## 4. Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with the X/Twitter website solely to facilitate the download of content you have access to. We are not affiliated with X Corp.
